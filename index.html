Home Made Chocollate Cake

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons flour
  • 4 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa
  • 2 tablespoons whisked egg
  • 3 tablespoon milk
  • 3 tablespoon oil
  • 3 tablespoon chocolate chips
  • splash vanilla or other flavoring - try cinnamon

Methods

  • Mix all dry ingredients
  • Mix all liquid ingredients
  • Now mix all ingredients togather
  • add your chocolate chips
  • put it in the oven (or microwave)
  • 1 minute for microwave
  • 20 min oven at 180
